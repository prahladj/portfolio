Augmented Vision
Case Study
01. Project Overview
Project Description

Today there is an immense volume of videos and images due to the prevalence of CCTV streams, smartphones and more. Augmented Vision can protect areas of interest live. It can be used to prevent security incidents in highly frequented public or private spaces but also to ensure frictionless access to restricted areas for authorized personnel, while protecting citizens’ privacy with the highest level of data protection. Augmented Vision analyzes hours and days of video streams and thousands of photos to help find subjects and objects of interest in postevent investigations. Augmented Vision is designed to make sense of all available video and image data, allowing operators to quickly create leads to solve crimes.

Project Goal

A product that will help a brand or company to reach out to their targeted customers. When investigating a suspect you need to leverage all sources, including processing and analyzing a large variety and quantity of video and image formats. Once Augmented Vision has been fed the video and image data, it recognizes, records, and classifies persons and objects of interest such as silhouettes, faces, vehicles and license plates. This automatically improves suspect traceability and enhances associations that were previously unknown.

02. Problem Statement
Statement
Detect and Identify a person of interest Secure access for authorized personnel Detect suspicious objects and alert Detect and recognize a person and identify a color filter Detect and warn of an intrusion Speed up post-event investigations
Solution
Analyze videos or images from footage and run the video analytics to identify or detect a person of interest Detection of suspicious objects and alert the concerned personnel with a color filter Warning an intrusion and reporting thru analytics tools
