Friends Trust
Case Study
01. Project Overview

Friends Trust is a non-governmental organization dedicated to improving the lives of women and children facing challenges that hinder their access to essential resources. By partnering with various organizations, it promotes educational initiatives aimed at empowering individuals to meet their needs, pursue their aspirations, and enhance their quality of life.

Friends Trust established in 2025, initially supported three children in their educational, sports and cultural activities and has since expanded its reach, positively impacting women and children through diverse projects focused on education and women’s empowerment. Located on Vikekananda Nagar, Gokul Road in Hubballi and KSRTC Layout in Uttarahalli, Bangalore, the organization strives to help individuals realize their dreams and achieve a brighter future.

02. My role and responsibility

The process of developing a website begins with a thorough collection of requirements, which involves engaging with stakeholders to understand their needs and expectations.

Following this, a comprehensive competitor analysis is conducted to identify strengths, and weaknesses of rival websites, which provides valuable insights into market trends and user preferences. Based on the findings from these analyses, a series of design proposals are crafted, showcasing various layouts, color schemes, and functionalities that align with the project's objectives.

Once the design is finalized and approved, the actual construction of the website commences, where I will be translating the design into a functional platform, ensuring that it is user-friendly, responsive, and optimized for performance.
