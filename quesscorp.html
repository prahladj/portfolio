QuessCorp
Case Study
01. Project Overview
Project Description

It is a software platform that helps QuessCorp to manage employee-related tasks, from recruitment to offboarding. It automates administrative processes, centralizes employee data, and provides tools for functions like performance tracking, attendance and leave management, and payroll.

Project Goal

The main goal of an HRMS portal is to streamline HR operations and empower employees by centralizing HR functions in a secure, self-service platform. This leads to increased efficiency and accuracy, reduced costs, and a better employee experience through easier access to information and tasks like leave requests, payslip downloads, and personal data updates. For HR departments, the goal is to automate routine tasks so they can focus on strategic initiatives like talent management and employee engagement.

02. Problem Statement
Statement

Our organization currently manages critical HR functions such as payroll processing, attendance tracking, leave management, and employee record-keeping manually using disparate systems (e.g., Excel sheets, physical files, or non-integrated software). This fragmented approach leads to significant human errors, data inconsistencies and security risks, time-consuming administrative burdens for the HR team, and a lack of transparency for employees. The absence of a centralized, automated system results in delayed processes, difficulty in generating meaningful reports for strategic decision-making, and overall reduced employee satisfaction and engagement, ultimately impacting organizational productivity and compliance with evolving labor laws.

Solution

To tackle the aforementioned issue, we are focusing on the design and development of a centralized system that facilitates continuous interaction between employees and Human Resource personnel. This system will be structured into four primary components, each serving distinct functionalities to enhance user experience and operational efficiency. By segmenting the system in this manner, we aim to streamline processes, improve communication, and ensure that both employees and HR representatives can access the necessary tools and information seamlessly.

Attendance Management HR Reports Leave Balances Peer Feedback and Management
